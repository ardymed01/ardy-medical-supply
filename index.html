— 01 / Services

Specialty care, tailored to you.

Every patient receives a personalized assessment and a device fitted with precision. Our team specializes in four core practice areas — each backed by trusted brand partners and decades of clinical experience.

Diabetic Foot Care

Medicare-approved therapeutic footwear and custom inserts for diabetic patients. We carry an extensive range of men's and women's options designed for comfort, support, and complication prevention.

Orthotics

Custom shoe inserts and braces engineered to improve comfort, alignment, and joint support. Our orthotics relieve pain and enhance mobility for foot, ankle, knee, and back conditions.

Custom Fittings Pediatric Available

Prosthetics

Over twenty years of crafting personalized prosthetics for above- and below-the-knee amputees. Our focus is restoring mobility, comfort, and independence — with continuing care as your needs evolve.

AK & BK Specialists Ongoing Care

Post-Mastectomy

Specialized products designed to support women after breast surgery. We offer breast forms, post-surgical bras, and compression garments — all fitted privately to enhance comfort and confidence during recovery.
